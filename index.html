Main Activities

Bicycle Rides

Gain an overview of Victoria Falls in just a few hours on this small-group bike tour, with hotel pickup and drop-off ensuring a hassle-free trip.

Canoe Safaris

This is a fantastic way to spend a day out on the mighty Zambezi River. Game viewing and birding opportunities await all.

Sunset Cruise

This activity is a river sunset safari done on the upper part of the mighty Zambezi river. The duration for this activity is 2 hours where pick up times depend on the season.

Helicopter Flights

Take an unforgettable helicopter flight over the mighty Victoria Falls. See the falls from several different angles and take in once-in-a-lifetime views.

Steam Train & Party

A classic steam engine whisks you past Victoria Falls and right over Victoria Falls Bridge on this tour from the Zimbabwe side of the falls.
